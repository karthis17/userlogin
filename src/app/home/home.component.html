Computer Networks

Computer networks are essential in today's connected world. They allow devices to communicate with each other and share information, making it possible for people to work together and access resources from anywhere in the world.

Internet Of Things(IOT)

The Internet of Things (IoT) is a term used to describe the interconnected network of devices, appliances, and machines that can communicate with each other using the internet.
